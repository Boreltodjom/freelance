A Culinary Journey

Founded in 2018, Savory Bistro brings together the finest seasonal ingredients with time-honored cooking techniques. Our chef, Marcus Laurent, trained in the kitchens of Paris and brings his passion for authentic flavors to every dish.

We believe dining is an experience that engages all the senses. From our carefully curated wine list to our intimate ambiance, every detail is crafted to create unforgettable moments.
